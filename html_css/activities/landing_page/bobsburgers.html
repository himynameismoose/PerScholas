Bob's Burgers

About the Show

Bob Belcher is a third-generation restaurateur who runs Bob's Burgers with his loving wife and their three children. Bob believes his burgers speak for themselves and isn't afraid to offer a variety of off-beat creations. Bob's wife, Linda, supports his dream but is becoming sick of the slow times, as the restaurant is constantly in danger of going out of business. Despite the challenges, which include consistent harassment from Linda's ex -- a health inspector -- Bob tries to keep the grill sizzling.

Meet the Cast


Bob Belcher

Who is Bob Belcher? Robert "Bob" Belcher Jr. is a third-generation restaurateur, and the main protagonist of the Fox animated series, Bob's Burgers. Bob runs Bob's Burgers with the help of his wife, Linda Belcher, and their three kids, Tina Belcher, Louise Belcher, and Gene Belcher.

Linda Belcher

Linda Belcher is the secondary protagonist of Bob's Burgers. She is a wife of Bob Belcher and mother of Tina, Gene and Louise Belcher . Linda is a tall woman with an red-orange v-neck shirt (Previously a button-up), blue jeans and large red glasses along with yellow shoes.

Tina Belcher

Tina "Danger" Belcher, the eldest daughter to father Bob Belcher and mother Linda Belcher. She is the older sister to Gene Belcher and Louise Belcher. Tina lives a complicated life on the cusp of being a teenager, hit with a rush of feelings for various boys.

Gene Belcher

Eugene "Gene" Belcher is the middle child of Bob and Linda Belcher. He is the only boy out of the three siblings. Gene is an aspiring musician and a prankster. He maintains very close relationships with both of his parents and two sisters, Tina and Louise Belcher.

Louise Belcher

Louise Ann Belcher is one of the three central protagonists (alongside Tina and Gene) of the animated sitcom Bob's Burgers. She is the scheming, irascible youngest child of Bob and Linda Belcher, and the mastermind of several schemes and pranks pulled with the help of her siblings, Gene and Tina.

Kuchi Kopi

Kuchi Kopi (くーち こーぴ) is a fictitious character that has appeared in many forms throughout the series. He has been seen as a book character, nightlight, toothbrush, carrier bag, a set of walkie-talkies and in Bob & Louise's fantasies. Kuchi Kopi is Louise's favorite toy; she has been known to go crazy without it.
